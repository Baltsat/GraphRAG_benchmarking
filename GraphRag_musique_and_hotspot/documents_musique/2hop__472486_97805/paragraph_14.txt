Carnival Splendor is the sole ship of the "Splendor" class, a smaller, modified version of the Concordia-class cruise ship, operated by Costa. She entered service on 2 July 2008. At , she was the largest ship in Carnival Cruise Line until "Carnival Dream" debuted in September 2009. "Carnival Splendor" is widely known for the November 2010 incident in which a fire started in the engine room, shutting down all electrical power to the ship.

Created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, Flash Thompson first appeared in "Amazing Fantasy" #15 (August 1962), the same issue Spider-Man first appeared in. He was a regular character in "The Amazing Spider-Man" series for its first four years, leaving the cast in "The Amazing Spider-Man" #47 (April 1967) to serve in the Vietnam War. Though he reappeared in the comic a few times while on leave, he did not return to the regular cast until issue #105 (February 1972).

Htike Htike Aung (; born 1 February 1995 in Myanmar) is a footballer from Burma, and a defender for the Myanmar under-20 football team and Ayeyawady United. He can play center back and right full back. In 2013, Ayeyawady United signed Htike Htike Aung. He played in Myanmar U-20 national team for 2015 FIFA U-20 World Cup in New Zealand.

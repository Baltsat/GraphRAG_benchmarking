Jamieson Kent Price (born April 28, 1961) is an American voice actor, best known for his deep and booming voice for numerous anime and video games. He is known as the voice of Largo the Black Lion in "Tales of the Abyss", Iron Tager from the "BlazBlue" series, the Count of Monte Cristo in "Gankutsuou", Ovan in ".hack//G.U.", and Galbalan, the main villain of "" and Milton Grimm from "Ever After High". Price also had a part in the 2000 movie "The Patriot".

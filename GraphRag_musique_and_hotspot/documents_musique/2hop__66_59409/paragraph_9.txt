``Crystals ''is a song written and recorded by Icelandic indie folk / indie pop band Of Monsters and Men. It is the lead single for their second studio album, Beneath the Skin. The single and album artwork was created by artistic director Leif Podhajsky. The song appeared in The CW's`` Dare to Defy'' promo and the first trailer for The Good Dinosaur. The song was also featured in the soundtrack for FIFA 16.

The team is an assemblage of independent superheroes who join together as the Justice League. The seven original members were Superman, Aquaman, Flash, Green Lantern, Martian Manhunter, Batman, and Wonder Woman. The team roster has rotated throughout the years, consisting of many superheroes from the DC Universe, such as Atom, Black Canary, Cyborg, Green Arrow, Elongated Man, Hawkgirl, Hawkman, Plastic Man, Red Tornado, Stargirl, Captain Marvel / Shazam, and Zatanna, among others.

Go, Man, Go!
 is a 1954 sports film directed by James Wong Howe, starring Dane Clark, Sidney Poitier, Ruby Dee, Patricia Breslin, The Harlem Globetrotters and Slim Gaillard.
 Clark plays Abe Saperstein, the organizer of the Globetrotters.
 Poitier's character is Inman Jackson, the team's showboating center.
 Breslin plays Sylvia Saperstein, the love interest, and Abe's daughter.
 Gaillard plays himself.

"Yes!
 PreCure 5" is the fourth "Pretty Cure" anime television series produced by Toei Animation.
 The story is about a group of five girls who have the ability to transform into Pretty Cure.
 They have been given this ability in order to collect the fifty-five Pinkies spread across the land and save the Palmier Kingdom.
 The series began airing in Japan from February 4, 2007 and January 27, 2008, replacing "Futari wa Pretty Cure Splash★Star" in its initial timeslot and was replaced by its direct sequel series "Yes!

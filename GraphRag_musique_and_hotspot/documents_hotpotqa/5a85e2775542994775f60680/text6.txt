United Red Army (実録・連合赤軍 あさま山荘への道程 , Jitsuroku Rengōsekigun Asama-Sansō e no Dōtei ) is a 2007 film written, directed and produced by Kōji Wakamatsu.
 It stars Akie Namiki as Hiroko Nagata and Go Jibiki as Tsuneo Mori, the leaders of Japan's leftist paramilitary group, the United Red Army.
 Akie Namiki was nominated for Best Performance by an Actress at the 2008 Asia Pacific Screen Awards.

The Artie Lange Show was an American sports entertainment radio show hosted by comedian Artie Lange, airing from October 2011 to April 2014 on the Audience Network, DirecTV, SiriusXM Satellite Radio and several terrestrial radio stations by Premiere Radio Networks.
 It originally launched as "The Nick & Artie Show" with Lange co-hosting with comedian Nick DiPaolo until DiPaolo's departure in January 2013.
 The three-hour show aired live from New York City from Monday to Friday at 10:00 p.m EST.
 From September 7, 2012, the show aired live on the Audience Network on Fridays at 10:00 p.m. EST from Tuesday to Friday.

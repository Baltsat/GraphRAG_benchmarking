Il Mare (; lit.
 "time-transcending love") is a 2000 South Korean film, starring Jun Ji-hyun and Lee Jung-jae, and directed by Lee Hyun-seung.
 The title, "Il Mare", means "The Sea" in Italian, and is the name of the seaside house which is the setting of the story.
 The two protagonists both live there two years apart in time, but are able to communicate through a mysterious mailbox.

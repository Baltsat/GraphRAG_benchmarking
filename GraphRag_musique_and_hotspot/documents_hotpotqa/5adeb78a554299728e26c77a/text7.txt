JUKI Corporation (JUKI株式会社 , JUKI Kabushiki-gaisha ) is a Japanese manufacturer of industrial sewing machines and recently domestic machines headquartered in Tama-shi, Tokyo.
 It is one of the leading industrial machine manufacturers.
 JUKI ranks as the no.1 sewing machine manufacturer in the world.
 Headquartered in Japan, the company currently has manufacturing facilities in Japan, China, Vietnam and markets its products in more than 150 countries on six continents.
 Up until 1988, the company was known as "Tokyo Juki Industrial Company, Ltd.".
 The company motto, which doubles as a customer creed is "Mind & Technology" (as in 'emotionally accessible technology').

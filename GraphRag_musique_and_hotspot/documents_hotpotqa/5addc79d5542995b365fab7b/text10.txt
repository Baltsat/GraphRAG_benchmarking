SpongeBob SquarePants: Original Theme Highlights is the first album of songs played on the Nickelodeon TV series "SpongeBob SquarePants".
 It includes tracks sung by the cartoon's characters: SpongeBob SquarePants, Sandy Cheeks, Patrick Star, Squidward Tentacles, and Plankton.
 Its total running time is 9 minutes and 9 seconds, spanning seven tracks.

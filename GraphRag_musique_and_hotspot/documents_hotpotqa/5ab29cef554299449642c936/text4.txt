Emeritus Professor Barry Ernest Conyngham, {'1': ", '2': ", '3': ", '4': "} , (born 27 August 1944) is an Australian composer and academic.
 He has over seventy published works and over thirty recordings featuring his compositions, and his works have been premiered or performed in Australia, Japan, North and South America, the United Kingdom and Europe.
 His output is largely for orchestra, ensemble or dramatic forces.
 He is an Emeritus Professor of both the University of Wollongong and Southern Cross University.
 He is currently Dean of the Faculty of the VCA and MCM at the University of Melbourne.

Keeley Rebecca Hazell (born 18 September 1986) is an English glamour model, actress.
 Hazell became one of Britain's most successful glamour models , working with brands such as Page 3, "FHM", "Loaded", "Nuts" and "Zoo Weekly".
 She has also made numerous television appearances and has, more recently, focused on her acting career, appearing in films such as "Horrible Bosses 2".

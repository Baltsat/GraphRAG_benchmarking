Jonathan Michael Goldstein (born September 2, 1968) is an American screenwriter, television writer/producer, and film director.
 He has written for numerous situation comedies, including "The PJ's" starring Eddie Murphy, "The Geena Davis Show", "Good Morning Miami", "Four Kings", and "The New Adventures of Old Christine".
 He is known for co-writing "Horrible Bosses" and "", and for co-writing and directing "Vacation" with his creative partner John Francis Daley.

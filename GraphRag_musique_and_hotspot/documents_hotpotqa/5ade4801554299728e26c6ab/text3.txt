Loopy is a 2004 film written and directed by Seth Michael Donsky.
 It is an adaptation of a short story by Ruth Rendell.
 "Loopy" screened at the Palm Springs International Festival of Short Films, the Cinequest Film Festival and the Clermont-Ferrand International Short Film Festival.
 "Loopy" currently airs in rotation on the Independent Film Channel.
 The tagline for the film is "A sheep in wolf's clothing!"

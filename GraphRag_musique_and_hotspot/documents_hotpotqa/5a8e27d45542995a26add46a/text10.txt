Marcus Birro (born 15 June 1972 in Gothenburg, Sweden) is a Swedish-Italian (Italian citizen) poet, author and columnist and former frontman of cult punk band The Christer Petterssons. Birro blogged at Expressen and was a presenter on at Sveriges Radio Östergötland, where he was the host of "Karlavagnen" on Sveriges Radio P4.
 He is the brother of author Peter Birro.
 Marcus Birro lives in Södermalm, is divorced from his wife of 4 years, they have two children together.
 In 2015, Birro told the press about his ongoing relationship with a married woman, Micaela Kinnunen, wife of politician Martin Kinnunen, and later Kinnunen confirmed the relationship and her divorce via her Facebook page.

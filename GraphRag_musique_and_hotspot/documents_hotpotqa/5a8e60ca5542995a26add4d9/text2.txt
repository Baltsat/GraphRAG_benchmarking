Danielle Nicole Panabaker (born September 19, 1987) is an American actress.
 She began acting as a teenager and first came to prominence for her roles in the Disney films "Stuck in the Suburbs" (2004), "Sky High" (2005) and "Read It and Weep" (2006), the latter alongside her younger sister Kay Panabaker, and in the HBO miniseries "Empire Falls" (2005).

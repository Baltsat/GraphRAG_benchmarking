The Housemaid (하녀, "Hanyeo") is a 1960 black-and-white South Korean film.
 It was directed by Kim Ki-young and starred Lee Eun-shim, Ju Jeung-nyeo and Kim Jin-kyu.
 It has been described in Koreanfilm.org as a "consensus pick as one of the top three Korean films of all time".
 This was the first film in Kim's "Housemaid" trilogy followed by "Woman of Fire".
 The film was remade in 2010 by director Im Sang-soo.

Marc Barthel (also known as Jesse D'Lane) is a singer, actor, songwriter and dubbing actor from Berlin, Germany.
 He was born on Oct. 04, 1989 in Berlin and first became famous as one of the lead members of the pop group ''.
 He left the group in 2007 to focus on his solo music career as 'Jesse D'Lane'.
 Furthermore, he has since then been working as an actor in television, film and commercial productions.

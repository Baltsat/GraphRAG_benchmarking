Love Love Love is the fourth album by Linda Chung, and was released on 13 November 2012.
 It contains 11 tracks, of which 3 are Mandarin while the rest are Cantonese.
 "The Moon Represents My Heart" is a remake of a famous song from the artist Teresa Teng.
 In an interview, Chung expressed that the album's main theme was the idea of happiness.
 Whilst at a promotional event for lovelovelove, Linda also stated that she has now saved enough songs to hold a concert.

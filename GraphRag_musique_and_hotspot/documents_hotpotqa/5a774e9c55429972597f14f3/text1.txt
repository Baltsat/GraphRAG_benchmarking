Karen Clark-Sheard (born Karen Valencia Clark; November 15, 1960) also known as "The Maestris Of Gospel" is an American gospel four-time Grammy Award-winning singer, musician, and songwriter.
 The youngest daughter of pioneering gospel choral director Mattie Moss Clark, Sheard began her career as a member of the legendary Grammy Award-winning female gospel group The Clark Sisters.
 She is the mother of contemporary gospel singer and actress Kierra "Kiki" Sheard.

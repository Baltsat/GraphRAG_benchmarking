flydubai (Arabic: فلاي دبي‎ ‎ ), legally Dubai Aviation Corporation (Arabic: مؤسسة دبي للطيران‎ ‎ ), is a government-owned low-cost airline with its head office and flight operations in Terminal 2 of Dubai International Airport.
 The airline operates between a total of 95 destinations, serving the Middle East, Africa, Asia and Europe from Dubai.

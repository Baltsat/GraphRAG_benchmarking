Sergei Aleksandrovich Kornilenko (Belarusian: Сяргей Аляксандравіч Карніленка ; Russian: Сергей Александрович Корниленко; born 14 June 1983) is a Belarusian professional footballer who plays as a striker for FC Krylia Sovetov Samara of the Russian Premier League.
 In Belarus, both Belarusian and Russian languages are official.
 Thus his name, usually transliterated as Sergei Kornilenko (Russian: Серге́й Корниленко ), can be alternatively spelled as Syarhey Karnilenka (Belarusian: Сяргей Карніленка ).

Michael Paul Stephenson (born February 28, 1978) is an American film director, producer, writer, and actor.
 He is best known for his starring role in "Troll 2" (1990) and for directing the ensuing documentary, "Best Worst Movie" (2009).
 His second documentary, "The American Scream" (2012), premiered on NBCUniversal's Chiller network and was named a "Must Watch" by "Entertainment Weekly".
 His latest film and narrative directorial debut, "Girlfriend's Day" (2017), is a Netflix original film.
 Premiering on Valentine's Day 2017, the comedy stars Bob Odenkirk, Amber Tamblyn, Natasha Lyonne and Stacy Keach.

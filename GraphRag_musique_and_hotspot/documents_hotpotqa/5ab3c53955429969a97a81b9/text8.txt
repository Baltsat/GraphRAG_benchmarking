This is the electoral history of Ed Markey, a Democratic Senator from Massachusetts.
 He was previously a Democratic Representative from Massachusetts, representing the 7th and 5th districts.
 Markey was first elected in a 1976 special election to replace the deceased Torbert Macdonald, and was re-elected in every subsequent election.
 He was also the Democratic candidate, and winner, of the 2013 special election, for the United States Senate.

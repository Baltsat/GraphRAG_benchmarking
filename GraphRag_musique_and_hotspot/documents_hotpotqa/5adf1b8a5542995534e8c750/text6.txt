 He also played as a member of the Canadian band Streetheart in the early 1980s, appearing on their "Live After Dark" recording, and joined several of his former Molly Hatchet bandmates in the band Gator Country in the mid-2000s.
 At his death, Crump was in the Jacksonville, Florida-based band White Rhino and the newly reformed China Sky.

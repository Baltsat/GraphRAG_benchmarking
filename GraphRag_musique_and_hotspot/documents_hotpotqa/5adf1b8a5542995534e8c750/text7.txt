WGNE-FM is commercial radio station that broadcasts to the Jacksonville area on 99.9 FM.
 The station is licensed to Middleburg to Renda Broadcasting.
 It is branded as 99.9 Gator Country and broadcasts a country music format.
 Its studios are in the Arlington district of Jacksonville, and the transmitter is in Downtown Jacksonville.
 Originally WIYD in Palatka marketed as "Wide FM", and sister to WWPF, AM 1260, and formerly "99.9 Froggy-FM" Daytona Beach, Florida, the station moved to Jacksonville, Florida in 2005.

Clean Head is Oceana's first EP, and a follow-up to their second release "Birth.Eater".
 The album was written with the intent of being the B-sides to "Birth.Eater" and covers similar topics.
 The record focuses primarily around the idea of finding beauty in life through whatever way you see fit.
 This album shows a vast sound change and maturity of the band as a whole and was very well received by fans and critics.
 Clean Head will be released as a Hot Topic exclusive, and on various online distribution services.
 This new EP has a total of four songs.
 The album was released on May 11, 2010. "
Birth.Eater" will also be re-released by Distort Entertainment, with the four new EP tracks.
 This is also the last release to feature guitarist Jack Burns as well as the last to feature the "Oceana" name.

Rory Kostjasyn (born 6 June 1987) is a former Australian professional rugby league footballer.
 He played in the National Rugby League for the Melbourne Storm and North Queensland Cowboys, with whom he was a member of their 2015 NRL premiership and 2016 World Club Challenge winning sides.
 An Irish international and New South Wales Country representative, he played at hooker, but could also fill in at lock and five-eighth.

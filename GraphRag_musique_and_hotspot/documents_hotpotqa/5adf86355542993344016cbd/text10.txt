Mike Nicholas is a former rugby league and rugby union footballer who played in the 1970s, and 1980s, and coach or team manager of the Wales Rugby League team since the 1980s, he is currently President of Wales Rugby League.
 He played club level rugby union for Aberavon.
 In rugby league he played for Warrington and the Cardiff Blue Dragons, and at representative Rugby League level for Wales and Great Britain.
 He played as a Front Row forward or Second Row, i.e. numbers 8, 10, 11 or 12.

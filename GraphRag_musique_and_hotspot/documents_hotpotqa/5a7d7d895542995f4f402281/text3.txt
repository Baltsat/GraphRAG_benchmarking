Mark Louis Recchi (born February 1, 1968) is a Canadian ice hockey coach and former professional player.
 He is currently an assistant coach for the Pittsburgh Penguins Recchi played 22 years in the National Hockey League.
 Recchi was a member of three Stanley Cup-winning teams, the 1991 Pittsburgh Penguins, the 2006 Carolina Hurricanes and the 2011 Boston Bruins.
 In Game Two of the 2011 Finals, he became the oldest player ever to score in a Stanley Cup Finals series.

Philip Drury Dawson (born January 23, 1975) is an American football placekicker for the Arizona Cardinals of the National Football League (NFL).
 He played for the Cleveland Browns from 1999 to 2012 and holds their franchise record for most field goals made, passing Hall of Famer Lou Groza in 2010.
 He played college football at Texas.
 As of the end of the 2016 NFL season, Dawson is the second oldest player in the league, behind fellow placekicker Adam Vinatieri.

The Prince and Me 2: The Royal Wedding is a 2006 romantic comedy film and the sequel to the 2004 film "The Prince and Me" and was released direct-to-video.
 Directed by Catherine Cyran, the film features Luke Mably reprising his role as King Edvard of Denmark, with Kam Heskin replacing Julia Stiles as Paige Morgan and Clemency Burton-Hill as newcomer Princess Kirsten of Norway.

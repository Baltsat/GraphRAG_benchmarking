True True Lie is 2006 thriller film directed by Eric Styles.
 The film follows Dana, who, after 12 years in an asylum, is reunited with her family and childhood friends Nathalie and Paige.
 Dana slowly begins to realize that the events that led to her stay there may not have been imaginary.
 "True True Lie" stars Jaime King as Nathalie, Lydia Leonard as Dana and Annabelle Wallis as Paige.

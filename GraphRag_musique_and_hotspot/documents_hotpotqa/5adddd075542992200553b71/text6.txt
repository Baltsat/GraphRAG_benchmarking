Gangland Undercover is an American factual based drama television series written and created by Executive Producer Stephen Kemp and co-writer Noel Baker.
 It was inspired by the story of Charles Falco a former ATF confidential informant (CI) who infiltrated an outlaw motorcycle club.
 The series is based on Falco's 2013 memoir, "Vagos, Mongols, and Outlaws: My Infiltration of America's Deadliest Biker Gangs".
 It premiered on Tuesday, February 24, 2015, on the History Channel at 10/9c.
 In Canada, Season 2 premiered on September 26, 2016.
 In the U.S., only the first episode of season 2 aired on December 8, 2016 on A&E.
 According to the trailer, "the new season" (Season 2) premiered on March 2, 2017.

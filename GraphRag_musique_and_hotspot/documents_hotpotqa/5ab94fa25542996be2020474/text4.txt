"Speed of Sound" is a song by British rock band Coldplay.
 It was written by all members of the band for their third studio album, "X&Y" (2005).
 Built around a piano riff, the song builds into a huge, synthesiser-heavy chorus.
 It was released by Parlophone Records as the lead single from the album.
 "Speed of Sound" was released in the US on 18 April 2005, and then made its radio premiere on BBC Radio 1 with Lamacq on the day of the release on 19 April.
 The single was pressed with two B-sides: "Things I Don't Understand" and "Proof".
 The song premiered in the UK on 23 May.

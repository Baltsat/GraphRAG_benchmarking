The "Three Colours" trilogy (Polish: "Trzy kolory" , French: "Trois couleurs" ) is a three-part film series directed by Krzysztof Kieślowski.
 Two of the films were made in French and one primarily in Polish: "" (1993), "" (1994), and "" (1994).
 All three were co-written by Kieślowski and Krzysztof Piesiewicz (with story consultants Agnieszka Holland and Sławomir Idziak) and have musical scores by Zbigniew Preisner.

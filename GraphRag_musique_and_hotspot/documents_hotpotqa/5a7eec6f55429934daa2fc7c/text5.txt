Haandbryggeriet is a Norwegian brewery founded in 2005 by Jens Maudal, Rune Eriksen, Arne Eide and Egil Hilde.
 The brewery was situated at the site of an old textile factory in Drammen, then in a railroad yard, and now resides in an old industrial building.
 Their brewing equipment was bought used in England and has a capacity of about 900 liters per batch.
 Production in 2006 was near 40,000 liters.
 In 2012, production was expected to be approximately 350,000 liters, using a ,800 liter brewing equipment.
 In 2013, they upgraded yet again, to a 5,000 liter brewing tank.

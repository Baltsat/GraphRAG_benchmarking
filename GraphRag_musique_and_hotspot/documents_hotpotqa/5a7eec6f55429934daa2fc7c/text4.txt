The 2008 AFC Champions League Final was a two-legged football tie to determine the 2008 champions of Asian club football.
 Gamba Osaka defeated Adelaide United 5-0 on aggregate to take the title.
 The first leg took place on 5 November 2008 at 19:00 local time (UTC+9) at Osaka Expo '70 Stadium in Osaka and the second leg took place on 12 November 2008 at 19:30 local time () at Hindmarsh Stadium, Adelaide.

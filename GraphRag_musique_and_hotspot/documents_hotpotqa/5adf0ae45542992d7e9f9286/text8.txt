Navni Parihar (born 22 March 1966) is an Indian film and television actress.
 Navni Parihar acted in the film "Rabba Main Kya Karoon" in 2013 starring Arshad Warsi.
 Navni is married to Animesh.
 Navni is also playing an important role in "Bani - Ishq Da Kalma", which was earlier titled as "Gurbani".
 Navni, who has worked two decades in Bollywood, also played the role of Indira Gandhi in Shekhar Kapoor documentary series "Pradhanmantri", and 7 RCR (TV Series).

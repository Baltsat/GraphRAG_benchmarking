HMS "Hannibal" was a "Majestic"-class pre-dreadnought battleship built for the Royal Navy, and the sixth ship to bear the name HMS "Hannibal".
 The ship was laid down at the Pembroke Dock in May 1894, she was launched in April 1896, and commissioned into the fleet in April 1898.
 She was armed with a main battery of four 12 in guns and a secondary battery of twelve 6 in guns.
 The ship had a top speed of 16 kn .

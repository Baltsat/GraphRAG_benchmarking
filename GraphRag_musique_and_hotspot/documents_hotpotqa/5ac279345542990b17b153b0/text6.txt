The Associated British Oil Engine Company (ABOE) was a British engineering company.
 It started life as a combine, similar to Agricultural & General Engineers.
 Petters Limited joined ABOE in 1937.
 J&H McLaren & Co. was sold to ABOE in 1943, although it may have been a member from an earlier date.
 In 1945 Mirrlees, Bickerton and Day joined the group followed by the National Gas and Oil Engine company in 1950.

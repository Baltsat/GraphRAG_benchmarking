The 2013 Weber State Wildcats football team represented Weber State University in the 2013 NCAA Division I FCS football season.
 Jody Sears returned as the head coach for the 2013 season, after being the interim head coach during 2012, and will be working with a new offensive coordinator and defensive coordinator in Robin Pflugrad and Eric Lewis.
 Weber State played their home games at Stewart Stadium.
 They were a member of the Big Sky Conference.
 They finished the season 2–10, 1–7 in Big Sky play to finish in a tie for 11th place.

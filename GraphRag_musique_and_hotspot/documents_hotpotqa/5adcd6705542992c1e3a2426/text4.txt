Luigi Scevola (born Brescia, 1770 - died Milan, 1819) was an Italian dramatist.
 He wrote in the style of Ugo Foscolo, and was the author of the tragedies "Socrate" (1804), "Annibale in Bitinia" (1806) and "Saffo" (1814).
 One of his plays was the basis of the libretti for "Giulietta e Romeo" by Nicola Vaccai and "I Capuleti e i Montecchi" by Vincenzo Bellini.

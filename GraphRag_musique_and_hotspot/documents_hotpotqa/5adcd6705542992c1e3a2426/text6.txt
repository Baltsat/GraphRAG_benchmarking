Antonietta Marini-Rainieri was an Italian operatic soprano active during the first half of the 19th century.
 She was married to lauded operatic bass Ignazio Marini and often appeared on stage with him.

 She reprised that role in December 1843 at La Scala and at the Teatro Carlo Felice in February 1844.

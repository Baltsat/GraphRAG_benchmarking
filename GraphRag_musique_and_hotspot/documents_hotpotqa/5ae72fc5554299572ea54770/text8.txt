Daduwal (Punjabi: ਦਾਦੂਵਾਲ ) is a village in Rurka Kalan tehsil in Jalandhar district of Punjab State, India.
 It is located 12 km away from Phagwara, 36 km from Phillaur, 20.2 km from district headquarter Jalandhar and 140 km from state capital Chandigarh.
 The village is administrated by a sarpanch who is an elected representative of village as per Panchayati raj (India).

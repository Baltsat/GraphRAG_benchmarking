Siya Khoh is a small village in the Alwar District of Rajasthan, India.
 It comes under the Mundawar tehsil, the Basmenpur gram panchayat, and the Jaipur Division.
 It is located 45 km towards North from District headquarters Alwar, and 160 km from State capital Jaipur.
 The PIN code of Siya Khoh is 301407, and thje postal head office is Menpur.

Mehsampur (Punjabi: ਮਹਿਸਮਪੁਰ ) is a village in Jalandhar district of Punjab State, India.
 It is located 16 km from Nakodar, 31 km from Phillaur, 40 km from district headquarter Jalandhar and 140 km from state capital Chandigarh.
 The village is administrated by a sarpanch who is an elected representative of village as per Panchayati raj (India).

Angus James Robson (born 19 February 1992 in Sydney) is an Australian cricketer who played for Leicestershire.
 He is the brother of England and Middlesex opening batsman, Sam.
 He has appeared in 26 first-class matches as a right-handed batsman who bowls leg breaks.
 He was part of the Leicestershire side that completed a famous first victory in 3 years against Essex on 3 June 2015, playing a big role in the side as an opening batsman, scoring 120 and 71 in the game.

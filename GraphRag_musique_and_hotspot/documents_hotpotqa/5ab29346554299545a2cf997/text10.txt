Muddy Boggy River, also known as the Muddy Boggy Creek, is a 175 mi river in south central Oklahoma.
 a major tributary of the Red River in south central Oklahoma, is formed by the confluence of Muddy Boggy Creek and Clear Boggy Creek.
 Both streams converge at a location known as River Mile 24 in Pontotoc County, Oklahoma.
 It is a major tributary of the Red River.
 The river is inhabited by over one hundred species of fish.

The Tufts Observer, founded as the "Tufts Weekly", is an undergraduate student newsmagazine published at Tufts University.
 First published in 1895 Tufts' first student newspaper, the Observer is the oldest student publication on campus.
 The Tufts Weekly was renamed the Tufts Observer in 1969.
 Observer staff currently work out of the Media Advocacy Board (MAB) Lab, located on the second floor of Curtis Hall on College Avenue.

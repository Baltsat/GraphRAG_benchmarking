Elena Pampoulova (also Elena Wagner, Elena Pampulova-Bergomi, Bulgarian: Елена Пампулова , born 17 May 1972) is a retired professional tennis player from Bulgaria.
 She competed for Fed Cup of the International Tennis Federation (ITF).
 Elena's first tennis coach was her own mother, Bulgarian tennis player Lubka Radkova.
 Elena's father, Emilian Pampoulov, is also a tennis player.

 The first season ended its 23-week run on February 24, 2017, with a total of 115 episodes, and replaced by "Destined to be Yours".
 The second season premiered on August 14, 2017, replacing "My Love from the Star" and occupying the timeslot of "Mulawin vs. Ravena".

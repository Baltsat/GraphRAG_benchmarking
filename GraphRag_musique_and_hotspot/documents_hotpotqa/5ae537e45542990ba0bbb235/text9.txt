Andre Parker, known by his stage name Chink Santana, is an American R&B musician and producer from Washington, D.C. His achievements include co-producing "Ashanti", the award-winning debut album from the singer of the same name.
 He also worked on her follow-up, "Chapter II," and has recently co-written and produced tracks on "Judgement Days," the second album by English singer/rapper Ms. Dynamite.
 He was also part of the making of Jim Jones' album, "Hustler's P.O.M.E. (Product of My Environment)".

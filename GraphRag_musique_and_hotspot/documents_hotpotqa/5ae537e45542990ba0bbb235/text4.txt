"Rock wit U (Awww Baby)" is a song by American R&B singer Ashanti.
 It was released in May 2003 as the lead single from her second studio album, "Chapter II".
 The song peaked at number two on the U.S. "Billboard" Hot 100 and became her first international hit from her second album, reaching number seven in the UK and number 19 in Australia.
 The song was certified Gold by the RIAA on June 16, 2010.

Anna Kalata (born May 10, 1964, Milanówek, Poland) is a Polish politician, celebrity and occasional actress.
 She was a member of the populist Samoobrona party.
 In Jarosław Kaczyński's cabinet she was the minister of labour and social policy.
 She participated in the 12th season of Taniec z Gwiazdami (the Polish version of Dancing With The Stars).
 After losing 38 kg she appeared on the cover of Shape magazine.

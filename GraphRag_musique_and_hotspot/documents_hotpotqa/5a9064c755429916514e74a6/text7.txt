Binion's Gambling Hall & Hotel, formerly Binion's Horseshoe, is a casino on the Fremont Street Experience in Downtown Las Vegas, Nevada.
 It is owned by TLC Casino Enterprises.
 The casino is named for its founder, Benny Binion, whose family ran it from its founding in 1951 until 2004.
 The hotel, which had 366 rooms, closed in 2009.

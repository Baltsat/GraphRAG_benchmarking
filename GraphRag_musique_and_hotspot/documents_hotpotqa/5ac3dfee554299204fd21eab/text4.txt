Shamo (軍鶏 ) is a Japanese action manga series written by Izo Hashimoto and illustrated by Akio Tanaka.
 It was started to be serialized in "Weekly Manga Action" in 1998 and moved to "Evening" in 2004.
 It was discontinued in 2007 due to creative differences but returned in 2011 and ended in 2015.
 It tells a story of a boy who killed his parents and turned himself into a cold-blooded martial artist.
 The manga inspired a Hong Kong film adaptation that was released in 2007.

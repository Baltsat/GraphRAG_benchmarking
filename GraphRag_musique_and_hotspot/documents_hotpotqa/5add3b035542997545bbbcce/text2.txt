American Spring is Anti-Flag's ninth studio album.
 The album was released on May 26, 2015.
 The album marks the band's first release for Spinefarm Records after releasing their previous two albums on SideOneDummy Records.
 A lyric music video for the album's first single, "Fabled World" was released in March 2015.
 The second single "Sky Is Falling" was released a month later.
 On May 19, A music video for Brandenburg Gate was released.

Chapter 1 is the second extended play (EP) by American country music singer Kane Brown who is signed with Sony Music Nashville in early 2016.
 The five-song EP was released on March 18, 2016, as his first EP with the Sony label although he had an earlier independently released EP on his own label titled "Closer".
 "Chapter 1" is considered a prelude to his debut studio album scheduled later in 2016.

The Poughkeepsie Galleria (locally known as "The Galleria") is an upscale shopping center on U.S. 9 in the Town of Poughkeepsie, New York, located just north of Wappingers Falls, and is the largest shopping center in Dutchess County.
 The Galleria has an area of 1100000 sqft with two floors containing 140 shops and 14 restaurants as well as a 16-screen, stadium-seating Regal Cinemas theater.

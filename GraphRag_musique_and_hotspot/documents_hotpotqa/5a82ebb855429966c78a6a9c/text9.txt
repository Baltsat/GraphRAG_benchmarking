Bhakla is a Chhattisgarhi film released on 5 May 2006.
 This film is notable because Lata Mangeshkar sang a song in this film, which was her first and last Chhattisgarhi song.
 Composer for his movie is Kalyan Sen, who is notable music director in Chhattisgarh.
 He is son of the Famous Gwalior Gharana Musicologists Dr Arun Kumar Sen & Dr. Aneeta Sen, & elder brother of Shekhar Sen.

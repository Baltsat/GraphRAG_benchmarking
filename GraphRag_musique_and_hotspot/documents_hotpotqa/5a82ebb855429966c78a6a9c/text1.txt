Kanthari is a Malayalam comedy entertainment movie released under the banner of Sri Shirdi Sai Baba R Prabhukumar.
 Directed and scripted By Ajmal.
 "Kanthari" Mollywood movie star casts are Rachana Narayanankutty, Sekhar Menon, Subiksha, Rajshri Nair, Sreejith Ravi, Manav, Balaji and others.
 This movie songs and background score (music) composed by Arun Choudary, Gautham Rinil (BGM) and released in the date of 19/6/2015 (Jun 19, 2015)

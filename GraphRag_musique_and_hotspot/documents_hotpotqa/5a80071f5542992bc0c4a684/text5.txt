Lara Croft and the Temple of Osiris is an action-adventure game developed by Crystal Dynamics and published by Square Enix for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One.
 It is the sequel to the 2010 video game "Lara Croft and the Guardian of Light", and the second instalment in "Lara Croft" spin-off series of the "Tomb Raider" franchise.
 The video game was announced at E3 2014 on 9 June.
 "Temple of Osiris" stars "Tomb Raider" mainstay protagonist Lara Croft, voiced by English actress Keeley Hawes.

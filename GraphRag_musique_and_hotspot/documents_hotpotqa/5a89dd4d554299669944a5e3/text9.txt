Joseph A. Hardy Connellsville Airport (ICAO: KVVS) is a public-use airport located four nautical miles (7 km) southwest of Connellsville in Dunbar Township, Fayette County, Pennsylvania, United States.
 It is owned by the Fayette County Airport Authority and serves the south-eastern segment of the Pittsburgh metropolitan area.
 The airport serves the general aviation community with no scheduled commercial airline service.

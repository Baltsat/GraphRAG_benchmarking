Oran is an unincorporated community in southwestern Fayette County, Iowa, United States.
 It lies along local roads southwest of the city of West Union, the county seat of Fayette County, and west of the city of Oelwein, the largest city in Fayette County.
 Its elevation is 1,043 feet (318 m).
 Although Oran is unincorporated, it has a post office with the ZIP code of 50664.

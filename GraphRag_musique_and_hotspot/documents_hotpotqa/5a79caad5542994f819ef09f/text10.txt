Shoki Kasahara (笠原 将生 , Kasahara Shōki ) (born January 9, 1991 in Fukuoka) is a Japanese professional baseball player who last played for the Yomiuri Giants in Japan's Nippon Professional Baseball.
 His younger brother Taiga is also a professional baseball player currently playing for Fukuoka SoftBank Hawks.
 His father Eiichi is a former professional baseball player.

Matthew Ryan Kemp (born September 23, 1984) is an American professional baseball outfielder for the Atlanta Braves of Major League Baseball (MLB).
 He began his professional career in the Los Angeles Dodgers organization in 2003, and played with the Dodgers from 2006 until 2014.
 He played for the San Diego Padres in 2015 and 2016.
 He has been named to two All-Star teams and has won two Gold Glove Awards (2009 and 2011) and two Silver Slugger Awards (2009 and 2011).

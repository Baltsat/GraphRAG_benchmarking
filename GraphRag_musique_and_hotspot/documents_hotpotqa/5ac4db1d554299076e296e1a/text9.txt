Sir David John Cameron MacKay {'1': ", '2': ", '3': ", '4': "} (22 April 1967 – 14 April 2016) was a British physicist, mathematician, and academic.
 He was the Regius Professor of Engineering in the Department of Engineering at the University of Cambridge and from 2009 to 2014 was Chief Scientific Adviser to the UK Department of Energy and Climate Change (DECC).
 MacKay was well known as author of the book "Sustainable Energy – Without the Hot Air".

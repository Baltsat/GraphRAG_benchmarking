St. John of the Cross Episcopal Church, Rectory and Cemetery is a historic Episcopal church complex located at Bristol, Elkhart County, Indiana.
 The church was built between 1843 and 1847, and is a one-story, Gothic Revival style frame building.
 It has a projecting bell tower with octagonal roof and lancet windows.
 The associated rectory was built in 1830, and is a 1 1/2-story, rectangular, Greek Revival style frame dwelling.
 The complex also includes the contributing church cemetery.

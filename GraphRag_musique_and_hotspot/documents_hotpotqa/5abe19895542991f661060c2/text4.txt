Liaocheng (), also known as the Water City, is a prefecture-level city in western Shandong province, China.
 It borders the provincial capital of Jinan to the southeast, Dezhou to the northeast, Tai'an to the south, and the provinces of Hebei and Henan to the west.
 The Grand Canal flows through the city center.
 Its population was 5,789,863 at the 2010 census whom 1,229,768 lived in the built-up area made up of Donchangfu district, even though large parts remain rural.

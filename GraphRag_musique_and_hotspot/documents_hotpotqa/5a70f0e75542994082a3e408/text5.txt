Barton Childs (February 29, 1916 – February 18, 2010) was an American pediatrician and geneticist.
 He was born in Chicago, Illinois, and graduated from Williams College in 1938.
 In 1942, he received his M.D. from Johns Hopkins University.
 Following military service in World War II, he returned to Johns Hopkins for a residency in pediatrics.
 After a fellowship at Children’s Hospital in Boston, he returned to Johns Hopkins University in 1949, where he remained until his retirement in 1981.
 He remained a professor emeritus in the Department of Pediatrics at The Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine until his death.
